FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers-and indeed in the history of intermational football_was recordedon 11 April 2001,when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0.

This legendary match also brought global renown for Archie Thomspon,whose 13-goal haul set a new world record,which stands to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.

And though the defeat earned American samoa ignominy, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that it is now the subject of an acclaimed document, 'Next Goal Wins ', showing across the world.
